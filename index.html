Introducing TidyTrack

At MANIT, we believe in fostering a clean and hygienic environment that's conducive to learning and well-being. We understand the importance of a spotless washroom, not just for comfort but for the overall health and satisfaction of our students. That's why we're thrilled to introduce TidyTrack, an innovative mobile app designed to empower every student to contribute to the cleanliness and hygiene of our beloved institution, Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT).

TidyTrack isn't just another app; it's a commitment to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene across the MANIT campus. With TidyTrack, we put the power of cleanliness in your hands, making it easier than ever to ensure that our washrooms remain pristine.

Key Features of TidyTrack

  • User-Friendly Reporting: TidyTrack allows you to report the cleanliness status of any washroom on campus with just a few taps. Whether it's a minor issue or a significant concern, your feedback is invaluable in helping us maintain a clean environment.
  • Real-Time Updates: Stay informed about the status of your reports. TidyTrack provides real-time updates on the progress of cleaning, ensuring that your concerns are addressed promptly.
  • Encourage Others: With TidyTrack, you can encourage your fellow students to actively report cleanliness issues as well. Together, we can create a culture of cleanliness and responsibility at MANIT.
  • Regular Cleaning Schedules: TidyTrack helps ensure that all washrooms on campus adhere to regular cleaning schedules, making sure they are always fresh and hygienic.
  • Rewards and Recognition: We believe that your efforts to keep MANIT clean deserve recognition. TidyTrack offers rewards and incentives for active users who contribute to the cleanliness of the campus.

With TidyTrack, we aim to create a cleaner, healthier, and more pleasant environment for every student at MANIT. It's not just an app; it's a shared responsibility that empowers us all to make a difference.

Join us in the mission to maintain a pristine MANIT campus, and let's work together to ensure that our washrooms are always a source of pride. Download TidyTrack today and be part of the clean revolution at MANIT!
