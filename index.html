Frequently asked questions

Explore answers to common queries about our app, including troubleshooting, features, and how to get support. If you have additional questions, feel free to reach out to us through the "Contact Us" option on our website.

Tidy Track is an app designed for MANIT students and staff to report campus issues such as food, hygiene, water, electricity, and internet problems. It allows users to submit reports, track their status, and ensure timely resolutions.

Yes, Tidy Track is designed for both students and staff at MANIT. Staff members can sign up and report issues relevant to their responsibilities and roles on campus.

After logging in, navigate to the relevant section (Mess, Washroom, Water & Electricity, Internet, etc.) and fill out the report form. You can describe the issue, attach an image, and submit your report. You will receive updates on the status of your report through the app.

Once a report is submitted, it is sent to the relevant department or staff member for review and action. You can track the progress of your report through the app and receive notifications about its status until it is resolved.

To change your login information, please contact the admin directly through the "Contact Us" option available on the official website.

You will receive an update available message within the app whenever a new update is available, along with a download option to update your app.
